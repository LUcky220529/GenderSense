Understanding Gender Sensitization

Awareness and respect for all gender identities and expressions

Gender sensitization is the process of understanding and being aware of the socially determined differences between men and women, including how they're expected to think and act.

Our mission is to promote gender equality by challenging stereotypes, addressing biases, and fostering inclusive behaviors in all aspects of life.

Through education and interactive experiences, we aim to create a society where every person is respected and valued regardless of their gender identity.

Gender Terminology Glossary

Understanding the language of gender identity and expression

Gender Identity

One's internal, deeply held sense of one's gender as male, female, neither, both, or something else entirely.

Gender Expression

External manifestations of gender, expressed through one's name, pronouns, clothing, haircut, behavior, voice, or body characteristics.

Gender Equality

The state in which access to rights or opportunities is unaffected by gender.

Gender Stereotype

Oversimplified generalizations about the characteristics, differences, and roles of individuals or groups based on gender.

Non-Binary

A spectrum of gender identities that are not exclusively masculine or feminine, and thus outside the gender binary.
